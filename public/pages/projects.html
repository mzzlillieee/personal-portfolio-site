Web Scraper to Check Immigration Status

This program estimates USCIS application and petition processing time.

USCIS does a really bad job of keeping one updated on the progress of a case. Checking manually at https://egov.uscis.gov/casestatus/mycasestatus.do is a slow and tiring process. Instead, I wrote a program to check and scrap the USCIS gov website for 40,000 or so cases before and after my own receipt number, and then it'd spit out all the records into a well-organized CSV for further analysis.

Tech: JS, Node, Express, Cron
